Growing Food

The one issue with Mars is that it's soil is not the same as Earth's, people are afraid that we can't grow food on Mars. However there are solutions as there always is. Through many scientific experiments where the scientist would simulate Earth soil into Martian soil. Scientists have actually managed to grow food on the simulated Martian soil.The food also contained no harmful chemicals or metals and was safe to eat. Take a look:

"Researchers grew 10 vegetables and cereal crops, including peas (pictured) in soils designed to simulate the surface of the Moon and Mars. They found while the Moon crops struggled, those in the Martian soil flourished as well as those on Earth."

As shown in the pictures scientists have managed to grow food with Martian soil simulant. Martian soil simulant is basically Earth soil but replaced with all the chemicals and nutrients that Mars soil has.

As seen in the movie,"The Martian",Mark Watney was forced to use his own human waste as fertilizer to grow his potatoes on Martian soil. This is not at all far from reality because obviously we use fertilizer to give plants the nutrients they need to grow. NASA and SpaceX are using this concept To save space for cargo and money.
